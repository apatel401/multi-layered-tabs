64% of people in Canada report having a strong sense of belonging to their local community – increases slightly to just over 66% by 2014.

2009

16% of adults aged 18 to 79 are meeting the Canadian physical activity guidelines recommendations – increases to 18% by 2015.

2014

59% of Canadians rate their health as “very good/excellent” – rate remains steady since 2003.

2014

7.8% of Canadians have been diagnosed with a mood disorder, a steady increase from 5.3% in 2003 (most recent data available).
